Sapiens, by historian Yuval Noah Harari, is the entire tale of the human race in roughly 400 pages. It’s the history of all of us, ever. And here are 3 reasons why this is the first book I recommended to friends and family, whenever they ask me for a recommendation: It’s the history lecture we always deserved but never got, it touches on some of the most intriguing questions ever and there are a ton of good ideas to dig into.
